My Teaching Journey

Sunitha

My path to Educere Learning began with a simple realization—after 15 years in banking, my heart was calling me elsewhere. As a mother of two and someone who truly believes every child learns differently, I discovered my passion for education through hands-on teaching opportunities in schools and serving as a primary school governor.

I developed my own approach by applying kinaesthetic learning and deeply personalized plans that honor each child's unique learning style, and watched children flourish with this method. What started as my personal calling has grown into something bigger, but our philosophy remains the same—we don't just teach subjects, we nurture confidence and help children fall in love with learning. At Educere Learning, every child is seen, supported, and celebrated for exactly who they are.
